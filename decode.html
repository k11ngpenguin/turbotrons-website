We were awarded the Sustain Award due to our budjet and team manajemnet, as well as our project planning and how we worked together as a team. We were recognized as a team that will sustain for many years to come
Engineering

Robot Design

Our robot has a custom drivetrain with a belt-driven system that is attached to our mecanum gripforce wheels for robot movement, as well as a fully custom designed ramp for maximum capacity of artifacts as well as a chained transfer/intake made out of rubber bands and a custom hooded shooter with angle changing using an axon servo, a intertia wheel, and a 6000 rpm motor.

Software

Code

This year we have implemented the use of the control hub's internal imu with a gobilda pinpoint computer connected to two 4 bar dead wheel odometry pods and a limelight camera. We use our sensors for the localization and pathing of our robot as well as using PIDF concepts to tune our motors for maximum control.
