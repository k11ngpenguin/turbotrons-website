Join The Team

Apply

Are you in 7th - 12th grade and are intersted in the STEM/Robotics field? If so, you can apply to join our team, Turbotrons 23279. We compete in a yearly competition known as First Tech Challenge (FTC) where we are given different ways to score points and have to design,build, and code a bot to preform these tasks.

Qualities

What We're Looking For

You don't have to know anything to apply for our team. You can learn through our application process where we will guide you on what you need to know to join our team.

Passion for STEM
The Number 1 thing we want from applicants is to have a passion/motivation to grow and learn throughout the season.
Team Player
You should be able to respect everyone on the team and talk nicely about any problems/situations
Commitment
Our season runs September through April. We expect our team members to attend all of our regular meetings and competitions, as well as take initiative on their own tasks.
Growth/Offseason
We expect all team members to learn during the offseason from the mistakes made in the current season and aspire to learn more about robotics and expand their knowledge to better prepare for next year.
Opportunities

Team Roles

Whether you like writing code, building mechanisms, or connecting with the community, there's a place for you on TurboTrons.

Software
Program robot control & autonomy
  • Java programming in FTC SDK
  • PIDF tuning & motor control
  • Autonomous path planning
  • Sensor integration (IMU, cameras)
  • State machines & OOP design
Hardware
Design, build & maintain the robot
  • Mechanical design & assembly
  • CAD modeling (OnShape/Fusion 360)
  • Maintenance, Troubleshooting, and Repairs
  • Prototyping & testing
  • Performance tuning and reliability
  • 3D Printer tuning and maintainence
Outreach & Media
Connect with the STEM Community
  • Event planning & volunteering
  • STEM presentations at schools
  • Social media & video editing
  • Sponsor relations
  • Engineering notebook & documentation
Process

How To Join

We keep the process simple — fill out the form, meet the team, and see if it's a good fit.

01
Application Form
Fill out the form below with your info, interests, and what role excites you most.
02
Assignment
Once you fill out the form we will send you a document that consists of problems you need to solve depending on the role you applied for to show if you can learn, implement, and finish tasks given to you.
03
Interview
Have an Interview with our coach along with our hardware/software team depending on what role you applied for.
04
It's Offical!
You are now apart of FTC Team 23279 TurboTrons
Good To Know

Important Information

Meeting Schedule
We usually meet every Friday,Saturday, and Sunday during the season and in our offseason we meet on Fridays. However, during the summer we meet at least 3-4 times a week.
Time Commitment
We would like at least 10 hours of commitment a week, and this is including coming to the weekly meetings as well as doing your tasks at home. If you cannot come to a meeting, please let us know a week in advance with a valid reason.
Who Can Apply
Open to all students from 7th-12th grade in the Tri-Valley
Questions?
Reach out anytime at turbotronsftc@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
